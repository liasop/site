8.18.25

Monday, August 18th, 2025 Around noon we got sandwiches from Rhea’s deli and went to dolores with Nat and Claire. The deli was great, I’m surprised we haven’t tried it yet. They had a bunch of options, and I got the valencia st.

Later, I made shortbread cookies from Sohla’s cookbook to bring to Nana’s the next day. The recipe was very easy and I thought they turned out well, just a bit sweet. Luckily there are a lot of mods and different options you can do, I think next time I might cut back on the crumble and also use a more sour or bitter filling. Even better on day 2!

Its been stupid beautiful in the city recently, our summer is finally here. I watched the swallows flying around together from the back deck for a little bit.
